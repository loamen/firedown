Reading the comparison

  • Live cards are constant. Every variant shows the same coral active-download and peach playing-media cards. Pay attention to how each shelf-card variant relates to the cards above.
  • Variant 3 demonstrates the homogenisation risk: when all four cards have a full coloured surface, the live ones stop being the loud ones.
  • Variant 1 vs 2: in 1 the icon floats on a tinted surface; in 2 there's a chip on a tinted surface. The 2-layer version is the literal screenshot pattern but doubles up on brand colour per card.
  • Variant 4: see how the dark shelf cards land in light mode — that's the cost of always-dark.
