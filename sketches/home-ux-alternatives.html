My honest read

D is the least change for the most consistency win — bookmarks already has its one-tap entry on the cradle, so a Home card would duplicate it; the actual fixable problem is that the "more" popup is currently a second route to Vault (already a card) and Bookmarks (already in the bar). Shrink it to History + Settings + Quit and the home gets cleaner without moving anything.

If you do want a search button on the bar, B is the cleanest: a flat 5-slot bar with History promoted from popup to slot, and Bookmarks as a card with the favicon-stack trailing. The search action is missing from B by design — I kept arguing it's redundant with the toolbar.

C is the bold redesign but I think it overcommits: Downloads losing its badge slot on the bar is a real downgrade for users who rely on glance-able download progress, and the new-tab FAB doubles the new-tab access.

A is the version I'd build last — it ships the search/bookmarks swap but doesn't fix the underlying duplication question, and the "42 bookmarks" subtitle adds nothing.
